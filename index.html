Vision

Our Vision

We seek a world of hope that is inclusive and just, where all people live in dignity and security.

Our Mission

To save lives, enable social protection and defeat poverty.

Our approach

GRACIOUS focuses on developing the potential of marginalised women and girls to drive equitable changes through programmes which deliver quality healthcare, inclusive education, gender equitable and sustainable livelihood opportunities and disaster relief and preparedness. Cross-cutting themes like building resilience, inclusive governance and gender transformative change are layered into all the programmes, across each sector.
